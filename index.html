sina taham

Sina (Persian: سینا) born March 13, 1989. Better known by his stage name Taham (Persian: تهم). Is an Iranian experimental hiphop artist from Tehran. Most of his music has a dark mood theme where he talks with real or imaginary people/characters he knows in his life or sings stories about them. he also has a young , mad ,conscious also bored Rapper side who raps about himself and his views of how the life is. The owner(s) of the night is the name he choose to call the The combination of these two characters.He is also involved in Directing, Visuals and clothing projects of his own.
